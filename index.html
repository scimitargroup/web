    Who We Are

    Built for the Future of Industrial Chemistry

    Scimitar Infrachemicals Private Limited is a Hyderabad-based chemical manufacturing company incorporated on 7th August, 2025 under the Ministry of Corporate Affairs. With a focused vision to serve India's rapidly expanding infrastructure and construction sectors, we engineer high-performance chemical compounds built for precision and durability.

    Operating from Plot No. 1031, Sy. No. 302, IDA Jeedimetla, Shapur Nagar, Hyderabad, Telangana, we specialize in paints, coatings, tile adhesives, waterproofing chemicals, wall putty, primers, caulking compounds, and specialty formulations.

    Classified under NIC Code 20229, the company is registered with the Registrar of Companies (RoC-Hyderabad) and operates in compliance with all applicable regulatory and environmental standards.
